Brexit Hero


When the hit-circle reaches the hit-line press the corresponding key on the keyboard. Try to click as soon as the hit-circle is in the center of the other circle to maximize score. You are judged on your accuracy, 300 for the center, 100 for just out the center, 50 for the outer ring and -500 for a mis. You also lose 500 score for clicking a button that you were not meant to press. When you get less than 300 score for a click you lose health but when you hit a 300 you gain health. If you run out of health (right bar) you have to restart the level. The timer (left bar) shows the amount of time to survive in order to pass the level. With each level you complete the circles go faster, spawn faster, your health goes down faster and up slower. Complete all 6 levels to spell 'brexit' on the sidebar to win the game.
