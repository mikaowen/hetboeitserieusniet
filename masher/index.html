Masher

In masher you have to mash your spacebar in order to vote for brexit. There is one problem, there are also EU-voters that will reduce the amount of brexit votes. You have to vote for brexit faster then they can vote for EU. The bar on the side indicates the amount of votes you have. If you reach 100 brexit votes you will go the next level and get a letter from 'brexit'. Complete all 6 levels and spell brexit to finish the game. But with each level you complete it gets more difficult because speed that the EU-voters vote at will increase.
